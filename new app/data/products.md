## Introduction

Swiss Life is a leading provider of life insurance, pension, and financial solutions, enabling individuals to lead financially self-determined lives. This document provides a detailed overview of Swiss Life's consumer-oriented products, categorized by their respective families, and extensive details for each product.
Swiss Life is Switzerland's leading provider of comprehensive life insurance, pensions, and financial solutions. With a rich history dating back to 1857, the company has established itself as a trusted partner for both private and corporate clients, enabling them to lead financially self-determined lives.

## Company History

Founded in 1857 in Zurich as the Schweizerische Lebensversicherungs und Rentenanstalt cooperative, Swiss Life was the first life insurance company in Switzerland. Over the years, the company expanded its presence across Europe, establishing branches in countries including Germany, France, the Netherlands, Belgium, the UK, Spain, Luxembourg, and Italy. In 1997, the company transitioned from a mutual to a publicly traded entity and adopted the name Swiss Life in 2002. :contentReference[oaicite:0]{index=0}

## Corporate Structure and Governance

Swiss Life operates through several divisions:

- **Swiss Life Switzerland**: The core market, offering a range of life and pensions solutions.
- **Swiss Life France**: Specializes in personal insurance and asset management services.
- **Swiss Life Germany**: Provides pension savings and financial security services.
- **Swiss Life International**: Offers cross-border insurance solutions for high-net-worth individuals.
- **Swiss Life Asset Managers**: Manages assets for institutional and private investors.

The company's governance structure includes a Board of Directors responsible for overall direction and a Corporate Executive Board led by Group CEO Patrick Frost.

## Product Families

### 2.1 Life Insurance

- **Swiss Life Dynamic Elements Uno**: Combines a classic savings component with equity investments for an optimized balance of security and return.
- **Swiss Life Dynamic Elements Duo**: Offers a modular approach with both tax-qualified (pillar 3a) and non-tax-qualified (pillar 3b) options.

### 2.2 Pension Solutions

- **Swiss Life Premium Comfort Uno**: A unit-linked life insurance designed for individual retirement planning.
- **Swiss Life Premium Comfort Duo**: Combines pillar 3a and 3b for tax-optimized saving and retirement planning.
- **Swiss Life Calmo IncomePlan**: Provides guaranteed supplementary income to support retirement needs.

### 2.3 Investment and Savings Products

- **Swiss Life Premium Expert**: Unit-linked life insurance with active investment management tailored to a ten-year investment horizon.
- **Swiss Life 3a Start**: Combines pillar 3a tax savings with flexible savings target protection.

### 2.4 Real Estate-Linked Products

- **Swiss Life Immo Elements Duo**: A hybrid insurance product offering access to high-quality Swiss real estate investments.

### 2.5 Annuity Plans

- **Swiss Life Calmo Life Annuity (3a/3b)**: Provides guaranteed lifetime income tailored for early or regular retirement needs.
- **Swiss Life Annuity Plan**: Offers fixed-term guaranteed income.

## Detailed Product Descriptions

### 3.1 Swiss Life Dynamic Elements Uno

**Key Features:**

- Combines classic insurance (security) and equity funds (returns).
- Available for pillar 3b.
- Offers dynamic or constant premium splits.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Adjustments possible for premium split based on individual needs.
- Tax advantages for pillar 3b disbursements.
- Financial protection options like lump-sum death benefits and premium waivers.

---

### 3.2 Swiss Life Dynamic Elements Duo

**Key Features:**

- Combines pillar 3a and pillar 3b.
- Flexible premium split options.
- Multiple investment strategies: Income, Balanced, Growth, Equity.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Optimized mix of security and returns.
- Broad flexibility to adapt to life changes.
- Potential tax deductions under pillar 3a.

---

### 3.3 Swiss Life Premium Comfort Uno

**Key Features:**

- Unit-linked insurance for pillar 3b.
- Four professionally managed fund portfolios.
- Automatic maturity management.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Attractive earnings opportunities with low-risk investment options.
- Tax-exempt investment income under pillar 3b.
- Full flexibility to switch portfolios.

---

### 3.4 Swiss Life Premium Comfort Duo

**Key Features:**

- Combines pillar 3a and 3b provisions.
- Offers tax benefits for retirement savings.
- Managed by Swiss Life Asset Managers.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Tailored investment solutions for diverse risk profiles.
- Maturity management reduces market volatility risks.
- Flexible premium payments.

---

### 3.5 Swiss Life Calmo IncomePlan

**Key Features:**

- Guaranteed supplementary income for retirement.
- Combines payout plans with lifelong annuities.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Index-basket participation ensures returns even in volatile markets.
- Offers flexibility in payout start and end dates.
- Access to funds at any time.

---

### 3.6 Swiss Life Calmo Life Annuity (3a/3b)

**Key Features:**

- Guaranteed lifetime income.
- Offers immediate or deferred annuity options.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Tax-efficient structure, especially for pillar 3a.
- Bonuses for higher returns based on investment income.
- Flexible coverage for partners and beneficiaries.

---

### 3.7 Swiss Life Premium Expert

**Key Features:**

- Active investment management by Swiss Life Asset Managers.
- Participation in Swiss Life Funds III (CH) Expert 2034 I A.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Term-optimized investment strategy reduces risks over time.
- Exclusive access to cost-effective unit classes.
- Guaranteed minimum benefit for beneficiaries.

---

### 3.8 Swiss Life 3a Start

**Key Features:**

- Combines bank savings with flexible protection.
- Tailored to pillar 3a contributions.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Tax-deductible contributions reduce taxable income.
- Flexible allocation of savings between accounts and investments.
- Adaptable savings target protection.

---

### 3.9 Swiss Life Immo Elements Duo

**Key Features:**

- Invests in high-quality Swiss commercial and residential real estate.
- Combines unit-linked and classic insurance components.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Exclusive access to institutional-grade real estate funds.
- Tax advantages for pillar 3a premiums.
- Flexible reallocation options.

---

### 3.10 Swiss Life Annuity Plan

**Key Features:**

- Provides guaranteed income for a fixed term.
- Flexible start and end dates for annuity payments.

**Differentiators and Strong Points:**

- Bonus annuities increase potential returns.
- Tax-efficient income structure.
- Designed for predictable income during retirement.
